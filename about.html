Our Team

Meet the educators behind Code and Circuit Academy

Mr Jason

Founder & Lead Instructor

Jason is a graduate of Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM) with a degree in Electrical Engineering. With a strong foundation in both hardware and software, he has worked as a software engineer and brings real-world industry experience into his teaching. During his university years, Jason was actively involved in tutoring and robotics education for school students — sparking curiosity and confidence in young learners. Now, as the founder of Circuit and Code Academy, he combines his passion for teaching with technical expertise to help kids explore the exciting world of electronics and coding in a hands-on, beginner-friendly way.

  • USM Electrical Engineering graduate
  • Real-world software engineering experience
  • Veteran tutor and robotics educator
  • Founder of Circuit and Code Academy

Our Teaching Approach

How we make robotics education effective and engaging

Hands-on Learning

Our students learn by doing. Every concept is reinforced through practical exercises and real-world projects that make learning tangible and memorable.

Small Class Sizes

With a maximum of 8 students per class, we ensure that every student receives personalized attention and guidance throughout their learning journey.

Project-Based Curriculum

Our curriculum is built around exciting projects that progressively build skills and knowledge, keeping students motivated and engaged.

Encouraging Creativity

We emphasize creative problem-solving and innovation, encouraging students to develop their own unique solutions and projects.
